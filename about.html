Our Acrylic Sheets are lightweight and only half the weight of glass without compromising strength, This industrial plastic is often used to make chemical storage tanks, oil tanks, and photographic developing instruments. Our wide range of high-quality polycarbonate embossed rolls and sheets can be used for various applications such as for veranda glazing, safety, or vandalized areas indoor/outdoor signs or instrument panels. They have exceptional impact strength and are unbreakable.
